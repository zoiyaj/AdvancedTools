About me

Master Stylist, Children's Author & Personal Trainer


Zoiya Jesani's love for her Canadian hometown, Richmond, British Columbia and it's beauty in the fall helped inspire her to write "Pitter-Patter Raindrops." As a Master Hairstylist who went part-time after becoming a mother of two, Zoiya returned to her joy of writing. Her children, who like many, needed extra soothing at bedtime. "Pitter-Patter Raindrops," is a calming children's bedtime story with the sounds and scenes that balance her city and it's nature. As her children grew up, Zoiya continued to chase more of her dreams and became a personal trainer. Now she plans to finish her courses with SheCodes to find the perfect work and home balance as a single mom.
